<p>The <a href="https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair" rel="nofollow">Boot-Repair</a> utility should be your best best to fix this issue.</p>

<p>To use it, you can either:</p>

<ul>
<li>download and boot on a <a href="http://sourceforge.net/p/boot-repair-cd/home/Home/" rel="nofollow">Boot-Repair-Disk</a>-<strong>64bit</strong></li>
<li>or boot on a Ubuntu-64bit disk, then install Boot-Repair in it</li>
</ul>
