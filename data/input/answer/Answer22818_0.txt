<p>Start the <strong>System Settings</strong> (e.g. via the button in the top right corner -> System Settings) and the go to <strong>Mouse and Touchpad</strong>. There select Touchpad. This tab offers you to enable two-finger scrolling and also horizontal scrolling.</p>
