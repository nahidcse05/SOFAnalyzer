<p>For the moment, use this as your <code>sources.list</code>:</p>

<pre><code>deb http://[cc].archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/ trusty main multiverse universe restricted
deb http://[cc].archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/ trusty-updates main multiverse universe restricted
deb http://[cc].archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/ trusty-security main multiverse universe restricted
</code></pre>

<p>Replace <code>[cc]</code> with your country code (such as <code>us</code> for the US, <code>fr</code> for France, <code>in</code> for India). And remove the channels you don't need (one or more of <code>multiverse</code>, <code>universe</code>, <code>restricted</code>).</p>
