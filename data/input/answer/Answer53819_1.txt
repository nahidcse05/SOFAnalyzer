<p>The question has been answered here:
<a href="http://askubuntu.com/questions/62249/how-do-you-change-brightness-color-and-sharpness-from-command-line">How do you change brightness, color and sharpness from command line?</a></p>

<p>On my Dell laptop:</p>

<pre><code>xrandr --output LVDS1 --brightness 0.8
</code></pre>

<p>You can run the <code>xrandr</code> command to see the name of your screens.</p>
