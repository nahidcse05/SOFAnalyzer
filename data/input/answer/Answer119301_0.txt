<p>How to install or fix your flash installation in Linux Mint</p>

<ol>
<li>Open Synaptic ; under administration menu</li>
<li>Search 'Adobe'</li>
<li>find, scroll down to, Adobe Flash Player plugin installer, which is titled 'flashplugin-installer'</li>
<li>right click and click 'mark for installation', or double click</li>
<li>click 'apply' in the toolbar</li>
</ol>

<p>if it is already installed, then right click and click re-install</p>

<p>if that doesn't fix it, then check to see what other 'flash' files are installed; scroll down</p>

<p>only 'mint-flashplugin-11' should be installed, uninstall everything else
THEN
re-install 'flashplugin-installer'</p>
