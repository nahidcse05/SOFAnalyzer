<p>To find out the current firmware, just press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Alt</kbd>+<kbd>T</kbd> on your keyboard to open Terminal. When it opens, run the command(s) below:</p>

<pre><code>sudo lshw -c network
</code></pre>

<p>once the command runs look for the firmware version, under the Wireless interface.  See image below.</p>
