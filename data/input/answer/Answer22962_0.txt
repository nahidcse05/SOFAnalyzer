<p>Insert a Windows 7 installation disc. If you don't have one, use the manufacturer' s recovery disc(s). Select your language , keyboard layout, and country, and press next. Click recovery options in the lower left-hand corner of the window. Click command prompt. Run the commands <strong>fixboot</strong> and <strong>fixmbr</strong>. When they are finished, eject the disc and restart the computer. This will restart the computer to it's default bootloader.</p>
