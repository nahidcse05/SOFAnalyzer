<p>Try boot-repair by booting ubuntu from live USB.</p>

<p>Install boot repair --></p>

<p>sudo add-apt-repository ppa:yannubuntu/boot-repair</p>

<p>sudo apt-get update</p>

<p>sudo apt-get install -y boot-repair</p>

<p>Run boot-repair --></p>

<p>sudo boot-repair</p>

<p>Click on "Recommended repair" </p>
