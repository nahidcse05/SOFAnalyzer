<p>Go to terminal and type <code>sudo nano /etc/rc.local</code> . A window will pop out, and add <code>echo 5 &gt; /sys/class/backlight/acpi_video0/brightness</code> before the last line “<code>exit 0</code>″. Number <code>5</code> is the value of your screen brightness. With <code>cat /sys/class/backlight/acpi_video0/max_brightness</code> command you can check the maximum. This might work for you, Have fun.</p>
